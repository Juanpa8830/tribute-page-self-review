The Amazing Leo Messi!!!

The man who became the best soccer player worldwide

(Lionel Andrés Messi; Rosario, Santa Fe, 1987) Argentine soccer player. Possessor of exceptional technical ability, devilish speed and inexhaustible inventiveness, since he arrived at FC Barcelona with his parents, at the age of 13, he made a dizzying career through the different categories up to the first team, in which He made his debut at just 16 years old in a friendly match against Porto. In 2004, at the age of 17, Leo Messi played his first official match in the Spanish League. At the age of 18, his international consecration came to him: he was part of the Argentine team, champion in the Under-20 Youth World Cup in the Netherlands, played in 2005. But his real breakthrough came with the arrival of Pep Guardiola on the Barça bench: during the four seasons in which the coach led the team (2008-2012), his goalscoring statistics reached stratospheric levels, decisively contributing to Barça experiencing the best stage in its history by winning 14 titles out of a possible 18. Unanimously considered the best footballer of the moment, his individual talent has been recognized five times with the Ballon d'Or (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2015).
